PUMPKIN SOUP RECIPE

Pumpkin soup is like, literally my favorite food (asides from school lunch mac and cheese) so i wanted to share my recipe for the best PUUMPKIN SOOOOUP that youll eber have! just toss these ingredients into a giant pot mid-day, let it simmer umtil dinner time, and then blend it!

  • about 50g of butter and some oil
  • diced onions
  • a few garlic cloves
  • chopped up leek
  • chopped up celery
  • some sage
  • carrot
  • half a pumpkin
  • add a mix of 2 heaped tbsp of chicken stock + 2-3 cups of boiling water
  • bacon
  • milk or cream
  • salt and pepper
